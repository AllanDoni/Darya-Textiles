Darya's Journey – All the way to success!

  Darya Textiles began operations in the apparel manufacturing industry in 2017, based in the southern part of India. The intricate stitching of our history from the threads of hard work, expertise and dedication comes together, like a beautiful piece of cloth. Over the years, the journey of Darya Textiles as a renowned custom clothing manufacturer is thrived on meaningful tailor-made creativity, attention to changing trends and true understanding of local spirits. With perpetual attempts to perfect the art in an environment of steady creative evolution, we’ve allowed ourselves to expanse our product portfolio widely in knitting and woven.


  Starting with a simple idea of interlacing the comfortable lifestyle in casual clothing, we’re now skilfully transcending synthetic and creative imaginations into reality. With the neat understanding of our clients’ conceptual, we delved ourselves into the field of full-service garment manufacturing right from designing curated collections to prompt delivery.


  No matter where you are at in your business we are here to help. We’re equipped to work with well-established fashion brands and also with young start-up labels.

Vision

 To make a world of sustainable fashion which is comfortable and affordable by everyone, that resonates the steady creative evolution.

 To be a company of international standards, carving a niche for ourselves through our impeccable quality products in the textile industry.

Mission

 To offer a best-in-class service for ultimate customer satisfaction through our design driven culture and our craftmanship in creating curated collections.

 To be a pioneer in the sector with innovative technology and competitive edge on advanced applications accelerating our service excellence.
